Why SkinSafe

Explore how SkinSafe helps users understand ingredients and choose skincare more confidently.

What SkinSafe Does

SkinSafe helps users understand whether a skincare product may suit their skin type. It reads ingredient names, highlights supportive ingredients, points out risky ones, and gives an easy-to-understand overall result.

Website Highlights

01

Skin Type Based Check

Each result is adjusted according to oily, dry, combination, or sensitive skin.

02

Clear Ingredient Result

Ingredients are marked as supportive, caution, risky, or unknown in a simple format.

03

Useful Product Suggestions

Better alternatives are shown so users can compare and choose gentler options.
