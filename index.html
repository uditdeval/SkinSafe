Simple Skin Tips

For Oily Skin

Go for lightweight gel formulas, niacinamide, and non-heavy moisturizers.

For Dry Skin

Use ceramides, glycerin, hyaluronic acid, and gentle creamy products.

For Sensitive Skin

Pick fragrance-free and soothing ingredients, and always patch test first.
