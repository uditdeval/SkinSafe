Skin Type Guide

Understand what each skin type usually needs before checking any product ingredients.

Skin Types Overview

O

Oily Skin

Usually has extra shine and may be more acne-prone. Lightweight gel products often work well.

D

Dry Skin

Often feels tight or rough. Barrier-supporting and hydrating ingredients are usually better.

C

Combination Skin

Can feel oily in some areas and dry in others. Balanced products are usually the best choice.

Ingredient Suggestions

Best for Oily Skin

Niacinamide, salicylic acid, green tea extract, and light gel moisturizers.

Best for Dry Skin

Ceramides, hyaluronic acid, glycerin, panthenol, and creamy moisturizers.

Best for Sensitive Skin

Aloe vera, centella, oat extract, allantoin, and fragrance-free formulas.

Quick Routine Advice

1

Cleanser

Use a gentle cleanser that does not leave your face too tight.

2

Treatment

Add only one active at a time so your skin stays comfortable.

3

Moisturizer

Always support the skin barrier with hydration after treatments.

4

Sunscreen

Apply sunscreen during daytime to protect skin from tanning and damage.
